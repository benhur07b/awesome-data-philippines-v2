# Philippines Power Statistics

## About the data 
This data portal presents comprehensive power sector data including: installed & dependable capacity, gross generation by grid and by technology, electricity sales & consumption by grid and sector, and system peak demand.

## Where to get the data 
[https://legacy.doe.gov.ph/energy-statistics/philippine-power-statistics](https://legacy.doe.gov.ph/energy-statistics/philippine-power-statistics) 

## Who maintains the data 
The EPIMB - Power Planning and Development Division (PPDD) maintains the data portal.

*Is this dataset licensed under an open data license?* Yes

## How to access the data 
Access the portal via its website and directly download files by clicking the title and then the download button.
